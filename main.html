Flower photos

A flower, also known as a bloom or blossom, is the reproductive structure of flowering plants (plants of the division Angiospermae). They are known for their vibrant colors, diverse shapes and sizes, and sweet fragrances. Flowers come in a mind-boggling variety, with estimates suggesting there are over 300,000 different species in existence! They can range in size from tiny blossoms, like the forget-me-not, to giant blooms, like the Rafflesia flower, which can reach up to three feet in diameter.
